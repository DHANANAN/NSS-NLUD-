From the Leadership.

“
Prof. (Dr.) G. S. Bajpai
Vice Chancellor & Senior Professor, NLUD

The NSS unit, National Law University Delhi, acknowledges the support and guidance of Prof. (Dr.) G. S. Bajpai, Vice Chancellor, NLUD. We express our heartfelt gratitude for the faith you have placed in us, as this trust served as a strong source of motivation and encouragement in enabling us to execute our plans successfully. The consistent administrative backing and enthusiastic support provided by you instilled the confidence and resolve required to carry out our initiatives effectively. This support is clearly reflected in the successful participation of two volunteers in National Integration Camp and other proactive activities the unit participates. The activities undertaken during the semester were executed within an administrative framework that encourages structured planning, accountability, and holistic student development. Your support has enabled the unit to undertake and complete activities requiring sustained coordination and institutional facilitation.

“
Dr. Risham Garg
Registrar · National Law University Delhi

The unit gratefully acknowledges the steady administrative stewardship of Dr. Risham Garg, Registrar, NLUD. His clarity of process and timely facilitation ensured that NSS activities moved from plan to execution with minimal friction, reinforcing the University’s balance between rigorous academics and a culture of service. The committee recognises that institutional commitments of this scale rest on the quiet, methodical work of the Registrar’s office — and is deeply thankful for it.

“
Dr. Prem Chand
Programme Officer · NSS NLUD · Associate Professor

The unit sincerely thanks Dr. Prem Chand, Programme Officer, NSS National Law University Delhi, for his consistent guidance and academic oversight. His mentorship ensured alignment with the University’s educational objectives while maintaining a balanced approach to academic and extracurricular engagement, significantly strengthening the committee’s planning and execution.
